        Ten Model

        Ten Model specializes in Gunpla and full metal remote control tanks, standing out in the industry with 3D printing and custom solutions. Established in 2014 and based in Shanghai, China, we bring over 20 years of professional model-making experience.

        • Approach to Craftsmanship

        We pride ourselves on crafting one project at a time, focusing on quality over quantity. Each model undergoes weeks of preparation, with thousands of parts, all meticulously handmade. We paint, bake, assemble, and adjust every detail to perfection using top-quality tools and materials, ensuring historical accuracy. Additionally, we research and develop custom 3D-printed parts to create the most realistic visual effects possible.

        • Paint Process

        Our exclusive baking paint, developed specifically for Gunpla, ensures durability and a flawless finish. After painting each part, we bake it in a special oven to guarantee long-lasting results. The key to achieving such an exceptional finish is our thorough pretreatment process, which ensures that every part is properly protected. Our technique follows automobile-like painting standards, ensuring not only a perfect appearance but also the longevity and resilience of each model. For our metal tanks, each metal part is carefully cleaned and sealed with protective coatings, providing protection against rust and ensuring long-term durability.

        • Custom Modifications

        We offer custom modifications, including 3D-printed parts, which are researched and developed using advanced technology such as AutoCAD. This allows us to offer innovative, highly detailed models that stand out from the rest.

        • Sales

        We sell Gunpla, Radio Control Tanks, and 3D-printed parts that reflect our unique craftsmanship. Rare kits, as well as custom kits that have been checked, repaired, or overhauled by us, are available for sale. Additionally, we offer modification parts that we have designed and developed in-house.

        • Meet Tian-chan

        Our founder, Zhang Tian, also known as Tian-chan, has over 20 years of experience in modeling. With a humble approach to craftsmanship, Tian-chan is dedicated to continuously improving his skills. Outside of modeling, he enjoys photography and traveling, which inspire his attention to detail and unique perspective in the world of model-making.
